Mowing the Green

My story focuses on the drivers of deforestation in the Amazon Rainforest in Brazil and their global impact. Deforestation in Brazil is a major concern that affects the entire globe and recently, Brazil's government has taken new steps towards protecting the Amazon. Growing up reading the National Geographic magazine, I was motivated by articles that exposed me to the biodiversity of the Amazon. My primary audience for this narrative is environmental activists and government officials who would be interested in Brazil's future environmental and economic pursuits.
